I also had a research postion at York University Canada.

I completed my Ph.D from the Department of Mechanical and Mechatronics Engineering under the supervison of Professor Steve Waslander at the University of Watelroo , ON., Canada in the area of aerial robotics and controls. Prior to that I was in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo, where I completed my MAsc degree in the area of controls and robotics.
